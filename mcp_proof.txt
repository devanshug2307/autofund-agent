=== Lido MCP Server Demo ===

Available tools:
  • stake_eth: Stake ETH into Lido and receive stETH. Earns ~3.5% APY.
  • unstake_steth: Request withdrawal from stETH back to ETH via Lido withdrawal queue.
  • wrap_steth: Convert rebasing stETH to non-rebasing wstETH. Better for DeFi.
  • unwrap_wsteth: Convert wstETH back to stETH.
  • get_balance: Query current stETH and wstETH balances and total value.
  • get_rewards: Check accumulated staking rewards and yield estimates.
  • get_apy: Get current Lido APY and compare with alternative yield sources.
  • get_governance_votes: Query active Lido DAO governance proposals from Aragon voting and Snapshot.
  • monitor_position: Generate a comprehensive plain English monitoring report for your Lido position.
